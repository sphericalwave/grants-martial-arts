MMA Team

A young, hungry team competing under the FLA banner — mentored by MMA legend TJ Grant. This is where serious competitors are made.

The Team

Grant's MMA team is built around a core group of young, ambitious fighters competing under the Fighting League Atlantic (FLA) banner. These athletes train daily across BJJ, Muay Thai, and wrestling, bringing it all together under the watchful eye of coaches who've done it at the highest level.

The team is mentored directly by TJ Grant — former UFC #1 lightweight contender with a 21–5 professional record, 8–3 in the UFC. TJ brings hard-won knowledge from the top of the sport and transfers it to every athlete who trains here. Having a legend in your corner every day isn't something most gyms can offer. Grant's can.

Classes cover cage and fence work, takedown defence under pressure, ground-and-pound control, and intelligent sparring protocols that build real fight IQ. A background in BJJ and/or striking is required before joining the MMA team.
