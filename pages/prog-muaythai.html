Striking

Muay Thai

The art of eight limbs. Punches, kicks, elbows, knees, and clinch — trained by competitors who've fought at the highest amateur level.

What to Expect

Muay Thai at Grant's is technical-first. Classes are structured around proper form, timing, and combinations before power. You'll develop real striking skill whether your goal is fitness, self-defence, or stepping in the ring.

Beginner classes cover stance, guard, basic strikes, and footwork. Intermediate and advanced sessions focus on combinations, clinch work, pad rounds, and controlled sparring.

No experience needed to start. Hand wraps required — available for purchase at the gym.
