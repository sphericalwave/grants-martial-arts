Roll Like a Girl

Grant's women's jiu-jitsu team. Competitive, welcoming, and tight-knit. Train with women who take the craft seriously and lift each other up in the process.

The Program

Roll Like a Girl is built around Grant's women's jiu-jitsu team — a growing group of athletes who compete, improve, and hold each other accountable. Whether you've been training for years or have never touched a gi, this is a place where women develop real skill without the ego that sometimes comes with mixed rooms.

Classes are folded into the regular BJJ schedule, with the women's team training alongside and supporting each other through fundamentals, drilling, positional work, and live rolling. The culture here is tough but never intimidating — coaches and teammates actively create space for women to thrive at every level.

No experience required. Come in, try a class, and see for yourself why the women's team at Grant's keeps growing.
