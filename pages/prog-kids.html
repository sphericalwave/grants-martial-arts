Kids Program

More than martial arts. Kids learn discipline, respect, and confidence in a structured, fun, and safe environment led by TJ Grant and Alex MacDonald.

What to Expect

The kids program is designed to meet children where they are and grow with them. Classes cover foundational BJJ and movement skills, but the emphasis is always on character — showing up, trying hard, respecting classmates, and having fun.

Our coaches have extensive experience working with youth athletes and understand how to hold attention, build confidence in shy kids, and keep the energy positive for everyone.

Sibling discounts available. No martial arts experience required — just a willingness to learn and move.
